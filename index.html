Privacy Policy

Last Crown  ·  Effective date: June 29, 2026

Last Crown ("the App") is a single-player medieval card game developed by Sezgin Berk Özer ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data is collected, why, and how it is used.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information. All game progress (stats, hall of kings records, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays interstitial advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data

This data is collected by Google, not by us. You can learn more and opt out at policies.google.com/privacy. On iOS, you can limit ad tracking in Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

Purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase disables all advertisements and stops AdMob from being loaded.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase ($0.99) processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple's privacy policy applies to all purchase transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Analytics

We do not integrate any third-party analytics SDK (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel). No gameplay events or usage statistics are transmitted to our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data Security

Because all game data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's own security mechanisms. We do not transmit your game data to any server.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
